Executive Council

Camions of Care at UAB is a registered student organization established in the spring of 2016.
We are led by an Executive Council of undergraduate students, who are elected each year.

VP OF COMMUNICATIONS
Aileen Jong

As the VP of Communications, Aileen is passionate about spreading our message through various mediums, especially through digital media and graphic design.
She is currently a junior majoring in Philosophy along with minors in Spanish and Public Health.
In the future, she hopes to brighten smiles in the community as a dentist. You can find Aileen hiking Birmingham trails and perusing antique stores in search of creative inspiration.
PRESIDENT
Michelle Nguyen

Michelle is a junior double majoring in Neuroscience and Philosophy, along with a minor in Public Health.
As President and Founder of Camions of Care at UAB, Michelle is a strong believer in serving the underserved and advocating for greater awareness regarding women’s health. In the future, she aspires to go to medical school and study to become a practicing physician. Michelle is usually found in the local bookstore reading dystopian novels or writing poetry.
VP OF OPERATIONS
Clara Wan

Clara Wan is currently a Molecular Biology and Philosophy double major at the University of Alabama at Birmingham. She is part of the University Honors Program and the Early Medical School Acceptance Program. In her free time, Clara enjoys swimming with the UAB Swim Club and volunteering at the Mitchell’s Place in Birmingham supporting individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorders. In the future, she would like study medicine and become a practicing physician.
