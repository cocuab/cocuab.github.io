Executive Council

Apply for Leadership
If you are applying for a leadership position, it counts as your general membership application as well!
Applications are due by December 1st, 2015 at 11:59PM. Appointments will be announced within a week after the deadline.

President - Michelle Nguyen

  • oversee all executive board/general meetings
  • maintain contact between club advisors and USGA regarding official business
  • establish contact with local organizations or any non-UAB-affiliated persons
  • official spokesperson for the organization
  • manage/maintain BSYNC page and Facebook group

VP of Operations - Clara Wan

  • keep detailed notes of all meetings and other official business 
  • notify members via email and other outlets of all meetings and events
  • record and document all organization history through both notes and photos
  • create organization archive 

VP of Finance - OPEN

  • keep detailed spreadsheets of all received donations (money/supplies)
  • sends mail and checks to main organization 
  • keep up with all the club’s finances and collect dues from members

VP of Communications - Aileen Jong

  • responsible for publicity and public outreach
  • create and maintain social media platforms 
  • create and maintain webpage
  • advertise the organization and its events to the public

VP of Distribution - OPEN 

  • coordinate and organize drop-offs of care packages 
  • keep records on the names of partnering organizations for reporting/publicity purposes
  • check in with partnership contacts to gain feedback and check when they need care packages
  • create distribution schedule spreadsheet
  • responsible for tracking, reporting and sharing spreadsheets on number of care packages distributed 

Philanthropy Chair - OPEN

  • responsible for creating and coordinating fundraising events and donation drives for hygiene products
  • responsible for requesting sponsorships from donors

Health Education Chair - OPEN

  • create menstrual hygiene, personal hygiene and health curricula for educational seminars 
  • coordinate and host educational seminars for female students and general audience
